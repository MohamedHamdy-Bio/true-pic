About True Pic

Our Mission

True Pic is an innovative medical-biological web-based platform designed to provide real, high-resolution microscopic images of human organelles and tissues with scientific annotations and citations from verified references.

Our platform aims to support medical, biochemistry, and life sciences students and researchers by offering scientifically accurate visual resources that enhance understanding and visualization of complex biological structures.

The platform features a clean and interactive interface with labeled organelles, image-based explanations of cellular structures, and simplified representations of metabolic pathways.

Our Vision

In future development, True Pic will integrate 3D animations and realistic simulations of biological processes to enhance understanding and visualization. Our long-term goal is to build a world-class medical image repository with tools for exploration, simulation, and annotation.

We envision True Pic becoming an educational reference tool similar to NCBI or NIH resources, but with a stronger visual and interactive focus, serving as a bridge between textbook knowledge and practical laboratory observation.
